How to Use the Voila Price Checker

This tool allows you to search for multiple products on Voila.ca using your personal session ID.

  1. Enter your Voila.ca session ID in the field below
  2. Enter search terms or product IDs to find products (separate multiple terms with commas or new lines)
  3. View the product details and prices
How to Get Your Session ID:
  1. Log in to your Voila.ca account
  2. Book a time in the region you want to search
  3. Perform any search in the Voila.ca interface
  4. Open your browser's developer tools (F12 or right-click and select "Inspect")
  5. Go to the "Application" or "Storage" tab
  6. Look for "Cookies" and find the "global_sid" cookie value
  7. Copy the entire value and paste it below
Note: To change regions, you need to log in to your Voila account, book a slot in the desired region, perform a search in that region, then extract the new session ID.
